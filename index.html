Intro

Greetings

Based in North America and passionate about engineering solutions that supercharge corporate workflows. I've been knee-deep in the Managed Service Provider (MSP) and Vendor Management Systems (VMS) scene for over 8 years now, and let me tell you, it's been quite the ride!

My background? Well, it's all about Information Systems and keeping up with the latest tech trends. My goal? To make sure businesses are making the most out of their technology. Whether it's navigating the ins and outs of IT systems or championing the use of Software as a Service (SAAS) applications, I've got you covered.

What sets me apart? My love for learning. I thrive on challenges and see them as opportunities to grow. As a Solution Engineer, I'm all about thinking outside the box and coming up with the best solutions for my clients.

Looking back, one project that really stands out is my time at Pontoon Solutions. I had the chance to lead some game-changing initiatives that redefined how we use technology to drive success for our clients.

But hey, work isn't everything, right? Outside of my professional life, I'm a bit of a wanderer. I love traveling and soaking up different cultures. Oh, and did I mention I'm currently tackling a new language? It's a fun challenge that keeps me on my toes. And when I'm not jet-setting or hitting the books, I'm all about giving back to my community, especially the LGBT community. It's important to me to lend a helping hand where I can.

That's me in a nutshell – a problem-solver, a tech enthusiast, and a lifelong learner. Ready to take on whatever challenges come my way.
